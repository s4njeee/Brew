About Our Café

Where every pour tells a story of origin, craft, and community.

Rooted in the Roast, Shaken to Life

Brewora began as a single espresso cart on V. Goopio Street. Today we roast our own beans in small batches, blending single-origin profiles with house-made syrups and farm-fresh fruit to create shakes that honor both tradition and creativity.

Our baristas train for six weeks before pulling their first signature shake — because temperature, texture, and timing matter as much as the ingredients themselves.

See how we craft each shake →

  • Ethically Sourced

    Direct-trade beans from Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala.

  • Zero Waste Goal

    Compostable cups and a cup-return program at every location.

  • Community First

    10% of seasonal shake sales support local food banks.

Our Crafting Process

Four steps from bean to the perfect shake.

  1. 01

    Roast

    Beans roasted in 12 kg batches for optimal flavor development.

  2. 02

    Brew

    Cold brew steeped 18 hours for smooth, low-acid espresso base.

  3. 03

    Blend

    Fresh ingredients folded in at precise temperatures.

  4. 04

    Shake

    Flash-chilled and hand-shaken for a silky, aerated texture.
